Tokyo

Tokyo officially Tokyo Metropolis is the capital of Japan. Tokyo forms part of the Kant≈ç region on the southeastern side of Japan's main island, Honshu, and includes the Izu and Ogasawara Islands.

Modern Tokyo is categorized as an alpha+ city by the Globalization and World Cities Research Network. As of 2019, the population of Tokyo was estimated to be over 13.9 million, making it Japan's most populous prefecture. The metropolitan area is the world's most populous with over 38 million people as well as the world's largest urban agglomeration economy.
